Who am I?

I'm a young, motivated front-end developer born and raised on Oahu, Hawaii, and now living a few minutes north of Seattle. Growing up in Hawaii has taught me that hard work and some aloha spirit can go a long way. Mix that with the endless resources a developer can find here in Seattle, and I have all the tools I need to provide you with a seamless development experience.

My Mission

My goal with this platform is to enable small businesses and brands to compete with their larger competitors from an online presence perspective.

Whether that entails building a new website, renovating an existing one, or simply creating more traffic, I'm here to maximize any investment you make in me.

My Philosophy

There's a lot of other people and companies that can do the same things I provide. But where I stand out is in the details and customer service.
I've refined my process in order to give you the same first-class treatment I'd provide my own family, and at a flexible, cost-effective rate that not a lot of other companies are going to provide.
